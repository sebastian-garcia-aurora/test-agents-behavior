# Company Mission & Product Vision

## Mission Statement

To empower software teams with intelligent, autonomous agents that eliminate toil, accelerate delivery, and make high-quality software development accessible to every organization.

## Target Market

**Primary:** Engineering teams at growth-stage startups and mid-size companies (10–200 engineers) who ship software continuously and need to scale throughput without scaling headcount proportionally.

**Secondary:** Enterprise platform and DevOps teams that manage complex multi-team coordination and need AI-assisted orchestration across their development lifecycle.

## Core Problem

Software teams spend 30–50% of engineering capacity on coordination overhead, repetitive tasks, and context switching — not building product. As teams scale, this overhead grows non-linearly. Existing tools automate isolated steps but don't reason across the whole system.

## Product Vision

A multi-agent platform where specialized AI agents collaborate alongside human engineers — handling triage, planning, implementation, review, and deployment coordination as trusted team members, not just tools. Agents maintain persistent context, follow company governance, and escalate intelligently when human judgment is required.

**Three-year north star:** Any engineering team can onboard a full AI "bench" — CEO, engineers, reviewers — that meaningfully reduces time-to-ship while maintaining quality and auditability.

## Core Principles

1. **Trust through transparency** — every agent action is traceable, auditable, and reversible.
2. **Human in the loop** — agents escalate, never override, when stakes are high.
3. **Context is currency** — persistent memory across sessions makes agents genuinely useful, not stateless tools.
4. **Governance first** — approval workflows, budget controls, and chain-of-command are first-class, not afterthoughts.

## Success Metrics (Year 1)

- Teams ship features 2× faster with same headcount
- Agent-handled tasks require human intervention <15% of the time
- NPS > 50 from engineering leads
